ArXiv Paper Analysis

RAG System

Select any ArXiv research paper and ask questions about its content. The AI will:

  • Retrieve relevant information from the paper
  • Generate accurate answers to your specific questions
  • Provide citations from the original document
  • Explain complex concepts in simple terms
Example Questions:
• "What methodology did the authors use in section 3.2?"
• "Summarize the key findings of this paper"
• "How does this approach compare to previous work?"

Document Analysis

RAG System

Upload your own documents (PDF) and get AI-powered insights:

  • Ask questions about your personal documents
  • Extract key information from lengthy files
  • Get summaries of complex documents
  • Find answers without reading entire documents
Example Questions:
• "What are the main recommendations in this report?"
• "What were the contract payment terms"
• "Summarize the key points of this section"

How the RAG System Works

1

Document Processing

Your documents are securely processed and indexed for efficient information retrieval.

2

Question Analysis

The AI understands your question and determines what information is needed.

3

Information Retrieval

Relevant sections are retrieved from the document based on your question.

4

Response Generation

A large language model synthesizes the retrieved information into a clear answer.
